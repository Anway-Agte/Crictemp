Frequently Asked Questions

  • How it works ?

    Simply create an account with us , follow the topics that you hold dear and you are all set to go . Crack your knuckles and post away .

  • Which posts do you see ?

    Primarily , your feed will contain all the posts that are related to the topics you follow , e.g your favourite team , player , league , etc . Occasionally you could see a post not conforming to that pattern , which you can choose not to see .

  • Are there any restrictions on number of posts ?

    Simply enough , NO .

  • How does the ranking system work ?

    Your activity on the website helps you to improve your rank . The more you participate in conversations , the more people react to your posts , the better your rank becomes . Your rank then in turn helps your posts to reach a wider audience .
